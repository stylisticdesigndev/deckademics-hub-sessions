# Deckademics Student Guide

Welcome! This guide walks you through everything you can do in the Deckademics app as a student. No experience needed — just follow the steps in order. Each section explains **what it is**, **why it helps you**, and **how to do it**.

## Getting Started

### Creating your account
Before you can use the app, you sign up and wait for the school to approve you.

1. Open the app and choose the **Student** sign-up option.
2. Enter your email and create a password.
3. Submit the form. You will see a message that your account is **pending approval**.
4. An administrator reviews and approves new students. Until that happens, you cannot reach your dashboard — this is normal.
5. Once approved, log in again with your email and password.

### Setting up your profile
After your first login, the app asks you to complete your profile so your instructor knows who you are.

1. Fill in your name and any requested details.
2. Save to continue.

### Adding your photo (required)
The app will not let you reach your dashboard until you add a profile photo. This helps your instructor recognize you.

1. On the photo step, tap **Upload Photo**.
2. Choose a photo from your phone or take a new one.
3. Wait a moment while it uploads. If it fails, it will automatically try again.
4. Once the photo appears, continue to your dashboard.

### Installing the app on your phone
Deckademics works like a normal app once you add it to your home screen. This also makes notifications work better.

1. Open the app in your phone's web browser.
2. On iPhone: tap the **Share** button, then **Add to Home Screen**.
3. On Android: tap the menu, then **Install app** or **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Open the app from the new icon on your home screen.

### Turning on notifications
Notifications let you know about new messages, class reminders, and announcements.

1. From time to time you may see a pop-up asking to **Enable notifications** — tap it and choose **Allow**.
2. You can also turn notifications on any time from **Profile > Notification Preferences**.

## Dashboard

### Reading your dashboard
The dashboard is your home screen. It gives you a quick snapshot of where you stand.

- **Next Class** shows when and where your next session is.
- **Skills at a glance** shows how you are progressing.
- Tap any card to open the full section for more detail.

## Skills & Progress

### Understanding proficiency levels
Your overall level is shown as one of four words instead of a percentage: **Novice**, **Amateur**, **Intermediate**, or **Advanced**. As you improve, you move up.

### Viewing your skills
1. Open **Skills** from the menu.
2. You will see individual skills with green progress bars.
3. The fuller the bar, the more you have mastered that skill.

## Curriculum

### Browsing what you will learn
The Curriculum section shows everything taught at each level so you know what is coming.

1. Open **Curriculum** from the menu.
2. Browse the levels from Novice up to Advanced.
3. Read the topics to see what you will work on.

## Classes

### Viewing your schedule
1. Open **Classes** from the menu.
2. Your upcoming class blocks are listed with day, time, and classroom.

### Telling your instructor you will be absent
If you cannot make a class, let your instructor know in advance.

1. Open **Classes** and find the upcoming class.
2. Tap **Mark Absence** (or the absence option on the class card).
3. Confirm. Your instructor is notified automatically.
4. You can cancel the absence later if your plans change.

### The "Running Late" button
If you are on your way but behind schedule, you can warn your instructor with one tap.

1. On the class card, tap **Running Late**.
2. Your instructor (and backup instructor) receive an instant alert.

## Notes

### Keeping personal notes
Notes is your private notebook inside the app.

1. Open **Notes** from the menu.
2. Tap **Add Note**, type your note, and save.
3. You can edit or delete your notes any time.

### Saving messages and images to Notes
If your instructor sends something useful, you can save it for later.

1. In **Messages**, find the message or image.
2. Tap **Save to Notes**.
3. It now appears in your Notes for easy reference.

## Messages

### Chatting with your instructor
Messages is a simple chat with your instructor.

1. Open **Messages** from the menu.
2. Read your instructor's messages and type a reply.
3. **Note:** you can reply for up to 7 days after your instructor's last message. After that, wait for them to message you again.

## Announcements

### Reading announcements
Announcements are school-wide updates.

1. Open **Announcements** from the menu.
2. Tap an announcement to read it.
3. Tap **Mark as Read** or **Dismiss** to clear it from your unread count.

## Profile & Settings

### Editing your profile
1. Tap your photo (avatar) to open the profile menu.
2. Choose **Profile**.
3. Update your details and save.

### Notification preferences
1. In **Profile**, open **Notification Preferences**.
2. Turn email, text (SMS), and push notifications on or off to suit you.

## Help

### Reporting a problem
If something is not working, tell the school so they can fix it.

1. Use the **Report a Bug** option.
2. Describe what happened. An administrator is notified.

### Sunday Practice
The **Sunday Practice** link opens the Deckademics practice page in a new tab for extra practice resources.
