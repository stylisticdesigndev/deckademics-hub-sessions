# Deckademics Instructor Guide

This guide covers everything you do as an instructor in Deckademics. Each section explains **what it is**, **why it helps you**, and **how to do it**, step by step.

## Getting Started

### Creating your account
1. Open the app and choose the **Instructor** sign-up option.
2. Enter your email and create a password.
3. Submit. Your account is **pending** until an administrator approves it.
4. Once approved, log in with your email and password.

### Setting up your profile and DJ Name
Students see your **DJ Name**, so this is required.

1. After your first login, complete the profile form.
2. Enter your **DJ Name** — this is the name students see throughout the app.
3. If you leave it blank, your first and last name are used instead, but a DJ Name is recommended.
4. Save to continue.

### Adding your photo
1. On the photo step, tap **Upload Photo**.
2. Pick or take a photo and wait for it to upload.
3. Continue to your dashboard.

## Dashboard

### Your daily overview
The dashboard summarizes your day: upcoming classes, students, and anything that needs attention. Tap any card to jump to that section.

## Students

### Viewing your roster
1. Open **Students** from the menu.
2. You see the students assigned to you.
3. Tap a student to open their detail view (skills, notes, and more).

### Primary vs. secondary instructor
A student can have a **primary** and a **secondary** instructor. This is a backup system: if one instructor is out, the other still receives the student's alerts (such as running late or absences). Both instructors see the student and both get automated notifications.

## Classes

### Viewing and filtering your schedule
1. Open **Classes** from the menu.
2. Your classes are listed in order by day.
3. Use the **Day / Week / Month** filter to change the time range. **Week** is the default.

## Attendance

### Marking attendance
Attendance is taken once a week per class, in Classrooms 1, 2, and 3.

1. Open **Attendance** from the menu.
2. Find the class session.
3. Mark each student **Present** (green) or **Absent** (red).
4. Your selections save automatically.

## Skills & Curriculum

### Assessing student skills
1. Open a student from **Students**.
2. Go to their **Skills**.
3. Set each skill on the 0–100 scale using the slider. The green bar fills as you raise the score.
4. Changes save and the student sees their updated progress.

### Viewing the curriculum
1. Open **Curriculum** from the menu.
2. Review the topics taught at each level (Novice through Advanced) to plan your lessons.

## Notes & Messages

### Writing notes about a student
1. Open a student's detail view.
2. Add a note about their progress or anything to remember.
3. Notes you write appear in the student's unified notes feed.

### Messaging students
1. Open **Messages** from the menu.
2. Select a student conversation and type your message.
3. You can include links and images. Students can reply for up to 7 days after your most recent message.

### Automated alerts
When a student marks themselves absent or taps **Running Late**, both the primary and secondary instructor receive an in-app message and a push notification automatically — no action needed from you.

## Announcements

### Posting and reading announcements
1. Open **Announcements** from the menu.
2. Read existing announcements, or post a new one if you have permission.

## My Payment

### Viewing your payment ledger
1. Open **My Payment** from the menu.
2. Review your payment history and amounts recorded by the school.

## Profile & Settings

### Editing your profile
1. Tap your avatar and choose **Profile**.
2. Update your details, including your DJ Name, and save.

## Admin / Teaching View

If you also have admin access, you can switch between views. Use **Return to Teaching View** in the admin menu to come back to your instructor dashboard.
